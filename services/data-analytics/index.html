What We Build

We build analytics systems tailored to your business — not one-size-fits-all BI tools:

  • Custom Dashboards: Real-time metrics, KPIs, and visualizations designed for your workflows
  • Data Pipelines: ETL systems that pull from databases, APIs, and third-party tools
  • Reporting Tools: Automated reports, scheduled exports, and custom alerts
  • Business Intelligence: Historical analysis, forecasting, trend detection
  • Operational Dashboards: Real-time monitoring for support, sales, and ops teams

We use React, D3.js, PostgreSQL, Snowflake, and real-time streaming (Kafka, WebSockets). Your dashboards update live — no refresh button required.
